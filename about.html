    Our Story

    Snout To Tail started as two dog owners in Auckland city who were tired of how conventional and tired all dog products and services were becoming. Our co-founders Nancy Hing, and Kristin White had one aim back in 2008: to make the best of the best more accessible. It started as a whisper, a rumour that something was in the works; and that's when people started passing notes. These notes were not about school girl crushes, they contained the names of some of the top brands available for New Zealanders and their pups. It started as simply a sharing of knowledge, and has since expanded into New Zealand's biggest dog expo.
